Free the Devs

Break free from app store monopolies. Join the movement for a fair, developer-first OpenAI-powered platform that puts creators first.

Why This Matters

Independent developers and solo builders face unprecedented challenges in today's app ecosystem. It's time for change.

🏪 App Store Monopolies

Apple and Google control distribution with 30% fees, arbitrary rejections, and changing rules that crush small developers overnight.

💸 Unfair Revenue Splits

Massive platform fees eat into already thin margins, making it nearly impossible for indie developers to build sustainable businesses.

🎯 Algorithmic Suppression

Your app's success depends on opaque algorithms that favor established players and paid promotion over quality and innovation.

🔒 Platform Lock-in

Developers are trapped in ecosystems where platforms hold all the power, changing terms and policies without notice or recourse.

🤖 AI Revolution Gatekeeping

As AI transforms app development, the same monopolies threaten to control access to the tools and platforms of the future.

🔥 Innovation Stagnation

When platforms extract maximum value instead of enabling creators, groundbreaking ideas never see the light of day.

What We're Asking

Our campaign calls on OpenAI to create a developer-first platform that breaks the cycle of exploitation.

  • Fair Revenue Sharing: Maximum 5-10% platform fee, not the industry standard 30%
  • Transparent Policies: Clear, consistent rules with developer input on changes
  • Open Distribution: Multiple ways to reach users, not just one gatekeeper
  • Developer-First Design: Tools and APIs built for creators, not advertisers
  • Community Governance: Developer representation in platform decisions
  • AI-Powered Innovation: Leverage OpenAI's technology to empower, not replace, human creativity
  • Global Accessibility: Equal opportunity for developers worldwide, regardless of location or background
